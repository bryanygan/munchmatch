Profile
MunchMatch is a Tinder-style food recommendation platform that will curate a personal food option for the user based on your profile. You’re presented with various food options, ranging from thousands of cultures, dishes, and cooking styles. The program will find and match the perfect meal for you as the user swipes left or right on each option. Preferences like spice tolerance and allergies are taken into account, and dishes that disagree with your preferences will be filtered out automatically. Other filters include price point, location, and overall customer rating.
